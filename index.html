How to Use

  1. Select a Graph Type: Choose between Force Directed or Hierarchical visualization
  2. Load Your Data: Click "Directory" to select your Mirth Connect channels folder
  3. Process XML: Click "Load" to parse your channel XML files and generate the visualization
  4. Customize: Use the controls to adjust forces, distances, node sizes, and layout options
  5. Interact: Zoom, pan, drag nodes, and add custom nodes as needed
